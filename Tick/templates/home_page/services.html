    Account Types.

    Tick™ offers two types of accounts. A Private and an Organization account.
    In the Organization account, you get the Phonebook, Calendar, Text Messaging and an addition option of adding the Organization's Staff members.
    The Staff Members can also Sign In in the Organization's account and view the information though they can not change(edit, delete or add) any information to the account.
    For the Private account, all the Apps are available except adding Staff Members.
    NB: When you Sign Up for the account, you get 500 Units free for testing. With 500 Units, thats is to say 16 text Messages So Sign Up for your account today!

    Text Messaging.

    Tick™ Text anables you:
    To send as many Text Messages as you can afford to any part of the World at avery low cost,
    Get instant reports on the activities on your account (for both Private and Organization accounts).

    Calendar.

    Tick™ Calendar, lets you schedule events and sends you notifications proir to the D-day.
    An Event Priority, defines how many reminders you will receive.
    High ===> Tick™, sends three(3) notifications to you before the D-day, or
    Medium ===> Tick™, sends two(2) notifocations to you before the D-day, or
    Low ===> Tick™, will send ony one(1) notification before the D-day.
    An Event can be Public or Private:
    Public means that you can add people to the event and they too will also receive the notifications.
    For Private events, notifications are sent to only the account owner.
    NB: The notifications are by text messages and e-mails only.

    Phonebook.

    Tick™ Phonebook is an app that lets you back up all your contacts in the same palce.
    While saving your Contacts , you can specify the Group in which they belong to. Send Texts to every one in the Phonebook or send to a a specific Group.
    NB: To send to every one in the Phonebook, specify the Group as "all".
